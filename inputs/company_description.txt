COMPANY OVERVIEW
----------------
Zomato Limited is a technology-driven food services company incorporated on January 18, 2010,
under the Companies Act, 1956, as Foodiebay Online Services Private Limited. The company was
subsequently renamed to Zomato Media Private Limited in 2010 and then to Zomato Private Limited
in 2019. It was converted into a public limited company and renamed Zomato Limited on April 21,
2021. The registered office of the Company is situated at Ground Floor, 12A, 94 Meghdoot,
Nehru Place, New Delhi - 110019. The Corporate Identification Number (CIN) of the Company is
U74110DL2010PLC198141.

NATURE OF BUSINESS AND PRODUCTS
---------------------------------
Zomato Limited operates a technology platform that connects customers, restaurant partners, and
delivery partners. The Company's primary business segments are:

1. Food Delivery: The Company operates an online food ordering and delivery platform under the
brand name "Zomato". As of March 31, 2024, the platform had approximately 17.5 million average
monthly transacting customers. The Company operates in 800+ cities across India.

2. Hyperpure (B2B Supplies): The Company operates Hyperpure, a farm-to-fork ingredient and
kitchen supplies platform for restaurants. As of March 31, 2024, Hyperpure served over 56,000
restaurant clients across 10 cities.

3. Quick Commerce (Blinkit): The Company acquired Blinkit (formerly Grofers) in August 2022.
Blinkit operates a 10-minute grocery and essentials delivery service through a network of dark
stores. As of March 31, 2024, Blinkit operated 526 dark stores across 25 cities in India.

4. Going Out (Dining Out and Ticketing): The Company operates dining-out and entertainment
ticketing businesses under the Zomato brand, enabling customers to discover and book restaurant
tables and purchase event tickets.

GEOGRAPHIC PRESENCE
--------------------
The Company's food delivery platform operates across 800+ cities in India as of March 31, 2024.
Blinkit's quick commerce operations are present in 25 cities. Hyperpure B2B operations are
present in 10 cities. The Company previously had international operations in over 23 countries
but strategically exited all international food delivery markets by 2022 to focus on the Indian
market. The Company's registered and principal place of business is in New Delhi, India, with
operational offices in Gurugram, Haryana.

TECHNOLOGY AND PLATFORM
------------------------
The Company operates a mobile-first technology platform available on iOS and Android. The
Zomato platform facilitates over 1 million food delivery orders per day across India. The
platform uses machine learning algorithms for restaurant recommendations, delivery time
estimation, and dynamic pricing. The Company employs a large technology workforce and invests
significantly in platform reliability and data infrastructure.

KEY BUSINESS STRENGTHS
------------------------
- The Company is one of the two dominant food delivery platforms in India, operating alongside
  Swiggy Limited.
- The Company has a differentiated quick commerce business (Blinkit) which operates the dark
  store model for 10-minute delivery of groceries and essentials.
- The Company's Hyperpure business provides supply chain services directly to restaurant partners,
  strengthening restaurant relationships and reducing dependency on third-party suppliers.
- The Company achieved Adjusted EBITDA profitability for the first time in FY2024.
- The Company has a large and growing customer base with approximately 17.5 million monthly
  transacting customers as of March 31, 2024.

SUBSIDIARIES AND ASSOCIATES
-----------------------------
Material subsidiaries of the Company include:
- Blinkit Commerce Private Limited (formerly Grofers India Private Limited) — 100% subsidiary
  operating the quick commerce (Blinkit) business, acquired in August 2022.
- Zomato Payments Private Limited — a subsidiary holding a Payment Aggregator licence from the
  Reserve Bank of India (RBI).
- Hyperpure Supplies Private Limited — subsidiary operating the Hyperpure B2B ingredient
  supply business for restaurants.
- Zomato Entertainment Private Limited — subsidiary operating the dining-out ticketing and
  events business.
- The Company holds a stake in Shiprocket (a logistics technology company) as an associate.

AWARDS AND CERTIFICATIONS
--------------------------
- Zomato Limited is listed on BSE Limited (BSE) and National Stock Exchange of India Limited
  (NSE) since July 23, 2021, following its Initial Public Offering.
- The Company has received multiple employer recognition awards including being listed among

FUTURE STRATEGY
----------------
The Company's key strategic priorities are:
- Expanding the Blinkit quick commerce network by increasing the number of dark stores to achieve
  broader coverage in existing cities and enter new cities.
- Achieving and sustaining profitability across all business segments.
- Growing the Hyperpure B2B supply chain business to serve a larger number of restaurant partners.
- Investing in technology infrastructure including machine learning, artificial intelligence, and
  logistics optimisation to improve delivery times and customer experience.
- Expanding the Going Out business (dining, ticketing, events) to grow the non-delivery revenue
  stream and increase platform engagement.
- Exploring adjacencies in the food and grocery supply chain ecosystem that are capital-efficient
  and synergistic with the existing platform.
